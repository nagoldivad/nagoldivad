About me

My name is David Logan. I create websites that are powerful and beautiful.

Whether you want a brand new website or want your old site redesigned, I expertly handle the design, hosting, security, and SEO (search engine optimization) that you and your business need to succeed on the web.

If you have any questions please contact me. While you're here take a look at my portfolio.

Web design is my passion

My goal is to build websites that are fast, beautiful, and mobile first. I specialise in web sites that look great on any device, and that are documented and maintainable. I can work with any widely supported CMS, such as WordPress. I can create custom themes and hand-built plugins. I can also perform systems administration and security tasks.
